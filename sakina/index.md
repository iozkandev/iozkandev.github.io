# Sakina

Welcome to the official support page for **Sakina**.
Sakina is a calm, modern companion for dhikr and prayer — count your dhikr toward per-prayer targets, check accurate prayer times, find the Qibla, and share Islamic message cards.

📲 **[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6752965489)**

---

## 📱 App Features

- Per-dhikr counting toward a target, with rounds and a live progress ring
- Two counting modes: the layered Orb and a swipe-to-count gold prayer-bead strand
- Dhikr library: built-in adhkar (Arabic, transliteration, translation) or create your own custom dhikr
- Messages: shareable Islamic greeting cards (Jumu'ah, Eid, daily quotes, Hadith, Qur'an verses)
- Prayer Times: accurate times for your location with a sun-path arc and next-prayer countdown
- Qibla Finder: live magnetic compass to the Kaaba with haptic feedback
- Day / Night themes across six accent palettes
- Available in English, Turkish, Persian, and Arabic, with full RTL support
- Works fully offline — all content is bundled, only prayer-time and Qibla calculations use your location
