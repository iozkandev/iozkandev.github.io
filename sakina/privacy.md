2. Location Data
Sakina uses your device's location for two on-device features only: calculating accurate prayer times for your area and determining the direction to the Qibla. This location data:

* Is used exclusively for local, on-device mathematical calculations
* Is never collected, stored, or transmitted to us or to any third party
* Is never linked to your identity or used for advertising, analytics, or tracking

Sakina works fully offline. All app content (adhkar, translations, message cards, etc.) is bundled with the app itself. Location is the only input that comes from your device, and it is used solely to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction on your device in real time. You can grant or revoke location access at any time in your device settings; if access is denied, prayer times and Qibla direction will not be available, but the rest of the app continues to work normally.
