# GymLog App – Support Page

Welcome to the official support page for **GymLog App**.

GymLog is a simple workout logging application designed to help you apply progressive overload and track your strength gains over time. Log your workouts, monitor your performance, and visualize your progress with exercise graphs.

---

## 📱 App Features

* Log workouts and exercises
* Track sets, reps, and weights
* View exercise history
* Monitor progress with graphs and statistics
* Simple and distraction-free interface
* Built with a focus on progressive overload
